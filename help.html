Frequently asked questions

How does CreditSniper work? +

Paste your credit report text into the app. CreditSniper analyzes every tradeline, identifies errors and violations, and generates dispute letters with real statute citations. You print the letters yourself (free) or we mail them for you ($1.99 regular, $4.99 certified).

Where do I get my credit report? +

Visit annualcreditreport.com — the federally authorized source for free credit reports from Equifax, Experian, and TransUnion. You can get all three for free.

Do you store my credit report? +

No. Your report is processed in memory and never saved to our servers. We don't store, sell, or mine your data. All processing happens on hardware we own — no cloud, no third party. We are healers only.

Is CreditSniper a law firm? +

No. CreditSniper is an AI tool that helps you prepare dispute documents. It cites real federal statutes (FCRA, FDCPA) and follows established procedures, but it is not an attorney and does not provide legal advice. Consult a licensed attorney for legal counsel.

How does the mail service work? +

After generating a letter, click "Mail It." We print and mail your letter via USPS through PostGrid. Certified mail ($4.99) includes return receipt — creating a legal paper trail proving the bureau received your dispute. Regular mail ($1.99) sends via First Class.

What is certified mail and why does it matter? +

FCRA §611 requires credit bureaus to investigate disputes within 30 days. Certified mail with return receipt proves WHEN the bureau received your dispute, which starts the 30-day clock. Without it, they can claim they never got it.
