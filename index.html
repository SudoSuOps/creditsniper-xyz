What CreditSniper does

Reads your report

Parses every tradeline. Identifies errors, late payments, collections, charge-offs, and inquiries.

Finds violations

Cites real federal statutes — FCRA §611, §623, §609, FDCPA §809, §805. Not templates. Real legal citations.

Writes the letters

Complete dispute letters ready to send. Account-specific. Legally cited. Addressed to the right bureau.

Score strategy

Tactical plan to raise your FICO. Utilization targets, authorized user strategy, rapid rescore timing.

Collector defense

Debt validation, cease and desist, statute of limitations defense, pay-for-delete negotiation.

Escalation paths

When bureaus don't respond — CFPB complaints, AG complaints, intent-to-litigate letters.

Your data stays yours

Your credit report is processed in memory and never saved to our servers. We don't build profiles. We don't sell data. We don't mine your information. Every analysis runs on bare-metal hardware we own — not rented cloud servers. No third party ever sees your report.
