Questions

What happens after my free trial?

Nothing. Your account stays active with the free tier limits. We don't auto-charge. You upgrade when you're ready.

Is CreditSniper a credit repair company?

No. We're an AI tool that helps you prepare your own dispute documents. We cite real statutes but we're not lawyers.

Do you store my credit report?

No. Your report is processed in memory and never saved. We don't store, sell, or mine your data. Ever.

How does the mail service work?

We use PostGrid to print and mail your letters via USPS. You review the letter first, then click "Mail It." We handle the rest.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts. No commitments. Cancel from your account page or email us.
